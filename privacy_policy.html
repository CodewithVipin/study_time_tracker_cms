1. Summary (Short)

Study Time Tracker stores your study timers locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. No account is required.

2. No Account, No Remote Storage

You can use the app without creating an account. All data—subject names and timer totals—is saved locally on your device using an embedded SQLite database. Nothing is uploaded to servers or cloud services.

3. What We Store Locally

The app stores only the information you explicitly enter or that is generated by the timers:

  • Subject names you create (text).
  • Total seconds recorded per subject.
  • Per-day aggregated study seconds (history) for reports.

These files are kept on your device's internal storage and are not sent anywhere.

4. No Third-Party Tracking or Analytics

Study Time Tracker does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any other third-party telemetry.

5. Permissions

The app does not request sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location. It only uses local storage necessary for the internal database and normal app operation.

6. Deleting and Resetting Data

You have full control over your data. Within the app you can:

  • Add, edit, or delete subjects.
  • Reset individual subject timers.
  • Reset all timers (via the Reset All control).
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device.

7. Children

The app does not intentionally collect information from children. No account creation or personal data is required.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to improve clarity or to reflect app changes. If the policy changes, an updated date will appear at the top of this page.
